There are many different options to play  
* videos   
* stacks of images  
* live streams  
in vvvv. See the [video](xref:7712d732-0b2e-4a27-bfb3-ef02f85f0794) section for details on the different options. In any case at some point the video will finally be available as texture which you can place on a [geometry](xref:7f070901-fd09-4087-a326-9e2e084ae8d5) in order to render it.

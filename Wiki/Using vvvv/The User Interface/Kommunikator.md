# Kommunikator

![](~/img/vvvv_kommunikator.png "")  



The Kommunikator allows you to preview, crop, save and upload screenshots to vvvv.org. If you provide your login you can even add images to the pool of site-headers.  

**How to open:**  
* **Ctrl+3**
